## A Commitment to Quality and Ethics

What sets Mother Tongue apart is their unwavering commitment to quality and ethical sourcing practices. Founded in 2019 by Jen Apodaca, a renowned roaster and champion in the coffee industry, Mother Tongue prides itself on sourcing exceptional coffees from producers they know and trust ‚Äì those who uphold the same values of sustainability, transparency, and fair compensation.

Through their genuine partnerships with farmers and importers, Mother Tongue ensures that every bean is ethically sourced and that the farmers and workers in producing countries are fairly compensated for their hard work and dedication. 

I have had the pleasure of meeting Jen a few times in the past and her passion for coffee and dedication to quality is truly inspiring. It's evident in every cup of coffee that Mother Tongue produces.
