## A Commitment to Quality and Ethics

What sets Cerebus Coffee apart is their unwavering commitment to quality and ethical sourcing practices. Established in 2019, this Southern Oregon-based roaster was born out of a desire to promote inclusion, conversation, and collaboration within the coffee industry.

Through their genuine partnerships with farmers and importers, Cerebus ensures transparency in farmgate pricing and consistently pays above Fair Trade minimums. They prioritize ethical practices and intentionality in every step of their operations, from treating cafe workers like family to sourcing unique and responsibly grown coffee varietals.
