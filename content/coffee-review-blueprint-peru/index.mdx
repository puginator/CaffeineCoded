## A Journey Through the Cup

The moment you take your first sip, you're greeted with a profile that's distinctly Peruvian, yet carries its own unique character. The cup presents a medley of flavors that evolve as the coffee cools:

- Prominent nutty notes form the backbone of the profile
- Delicate stone fruit adds complexity and depth
- Crisp apple acidity emerges as the cup cools
- White tea characteristics provide a clean, refined finish

What makes this coffee special isn't necessarily its boldness or uniqueness, but rather its thoughtful balance and reliability. It's the kind of coffee that becomes a daily companion, offering comfort and consistency with each brew.

- Rapid 2-hour evaluation and purchase process
- Above-market rates for quality coffee
- Quality-based price premiums to encourage improvement
- Direct feedback to farmers about their lots
- Support for small-plot farmers through the comit√© system
